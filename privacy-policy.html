Integration with Xano

Word Temple uses Xano as its backend service provider to manage user data, authentication, and app functionality. When you interact with the app, your data may be transmitted securely to Xano servers for processing. This includes:

  • Account creation and login: Your credentials are securely handled via Xano's authentication endpoints.
  • User progress and preferences: Stored in Xano's database to personalise your experience.
  • API requests: All communication between the app and Xano is encrypted using HTTPS.
  • Data retention: Xano stores your data only as long as necessary to fulfil app functionality or comply with legal obligations.

Xano does not access or use your data for advertising or profiling. All data is stored in compliance with GDPR and other applicable privacy regulations.

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.
